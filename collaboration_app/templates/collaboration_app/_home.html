Features

Discover the features that make Collabify the ultimate platform for students collaboration and projects success.

Group Creation and Management

Collabify simplifies the process of creating groups, allowing students to effortlessly form collaborative teams. By providing a user-friendly interface, users can easily create groups, invite members, and establish a centralized space for effective collaboration.

Task Assignment and Tracking

With Collabify, assigning tasks within groups becomes a breeze. Users can allocate responsibilities, set deadlines, and track progress, ensuring that everyone is on the same page and contributing to the project's success.

Deadline and Notification System

Collabify keeps users informed with timely deadline notifications. Students receive alerts and reminders for upcoming due dates, ensuring they stay on track and complete their tasks punctually. This feature helps in managing time effectively and avoiding last-minute rush.
